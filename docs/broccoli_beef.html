Broccoli Beef

instant pot
beef

Ingredients

Broccoli Beef

  • 2 pounds tri-tip
  • 1 pound fresh bite-sized broccoli florets
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Sesame seeds for garnish, optional

Sauce Mixture

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2/3 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 one-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated

Procedure

  1. Thinly slice tri-tip across the grain into bite sized bits
  2. Add Sauce Mixture ingredients to the pot and stir until well combined. Add beef and toss to coat.
  3. Pressure cook on high for 10 minutes
  4. Remove 1/4 cup of cooking liquid and mix with cornstarch (If you canâ€™t get 1/4 cup of cooking liquid out, supplement with water).
  5. Stir cornstarch slurry, broccoli florets and toasted sesame oil into the pot for 3 - 5 minutes until broccoli is tender and sauce has thickened, returning to SAUTE mode as needed.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds (optional). Serve over rice or zucchini Noodles.
